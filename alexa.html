Dallas Info (Official City of Dallas Alexa Skill)

Dallas Info is an Alexa Skill developed for the residents of Dallas, Texas, in association with Code for Dallas and the City of Dallas.

Features include:

  • What's my trash day?
  • Who's my council person?
  • When's the next city council meeting?
  • What's the status of my service request?

This list includes just a few of the skill's many abilities, and we are developing more features for this skill. On this project, I am serving as a software engineer.

Dallas Info is currently in beta testing.
