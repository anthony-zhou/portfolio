Dallas 311 (Official City of Dallas Alexa Skill)

Dallas 311 is an voice app that lets the residents of Dallas, Texas, access personalized city information. Since its release in August 2020, the skill has been downloaded by hundreds of Dallas residents, and featured on media outlets including Dallas Weekly and Dallas City News. In my position as a lead software engineer, I learned about voice interfaces, the Amazon Alexa Skills Kit, and civic technology.

Features
  • What's my trash day?
  • Who's my council person?
  • When's the next city council meeting?
  • What's the status of my service request?
  • When do I water my lawn?
  • ...and more!

Dallas residents with Alexa-enabled devices can visit Dallas 311's Alexa store listing to enable the free skill and try it out!
